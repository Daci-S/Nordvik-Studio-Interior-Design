Our Story

Design rooted in honesty and craft

We create spaces that feel entirely yours

Nordvik Studio was founded in 2012 by Emma Lindqvist, a Norwegian-born designer who moved to Sydney and found herself caught between two worlds: the restrained beauty of Scandinavian design and the warmth, light, and openness of Australian living.

That tension became a creative force. Our studio has spent over a decade refining an approach that honours natural materials, breathable spaces, and the particular quality of light that defines life in the Southern Hemisphere.

We are a small, dedicated team of designers and spatial thinkers. We take on a limited number of projects each year — not because we can't grow, but because we believe deeply in the quality of attention every space deserves.

Our clients are people who understand that a home isn't just functional — it is the stage on which a life is lived. They come to us ready to slow down, think deeply, and invest in something lasting.

"The best rooms don't shout. They breathe." — Emma Lindqvist

What We Believe

The values that guide every room

Restraint

We edit with purpose. Every object in a Nordvik room earns its place. Less, but better — always.

Materiality

We believe in oak, linen, stone, and light. Natural materials age gracefully and connect us to the world outside.

Function

Beauty without purpose is decoration. We design spaces that work for how you actually live — today and tomorrow.
