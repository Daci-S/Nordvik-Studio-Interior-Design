Service 01

Residential Interior Design

Our flagship service — a complete design journey from initial concept through to final styling and handover. We work with you to understand your life, your routines, and your aspirations, then translate that into a space that is genuinely yours.

Whether you're starting with a new build, renovating an existing home, or simply refreshing a beloved space, our residential design service takes care of every detail.

What's Included

  • Discovery consultation & lifestyle brief
  • Concept development with mood boards
  • Space planning & floor plans
  • Material, finish & furniture specification
  • Trade procurement & project coordination
  • Styling & final installation

Service 02

Space Planning

Great interiors begin with great spatial thinking. Our space planning service analyses how you move through a room, how light shifts through the day, and how layout can unlock the full potential of your floor plan.

We deliver measured drawings, 3D sketches, and detailed furniture layouts so you can visualise your space before committing to any changes.

What's Included

  • Site measurement & assessment
  • Multiple layout concepts
  • 3D spatial sketches
  • Furniture placement drawings
  • Traffic flow & functional analysis

Service 03

Furniture Curation

Choosing the right furniture is half the battle. Our curation service draws on years of relationships with Australian and international suppliers to source pieces that are both beautiful and built to last.

We present a curated edit — no overwhelming showroom visits — just a thoughtful selection tailored to your space, budget, and taste.

What's Included

  • Budget planning & range research
  • Curated furniture & object selection
  • Trade pricing access
  • Textile & soft furnishing specification
  • Artwork & accessory sourcing

Service 04

Styling & Staging

Sometimes a space just needs the right eye. Our styling service transforms existing rooms through considered rearrangement, layering of textiles, and the addition of carefully chosen objects and botanicals.

Also ideal for property sellers, our staging service ensures your home photographs beautifully and creates an irresistible first impression.

What's Included

  • Half-day or full-day styling visit
  • Accessory & botanical sourcing
  • Lighting adjustment & layering
  • Photography-ready presentation
  • Styling guide document
